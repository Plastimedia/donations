Why we're here

In Colombia, 400,000 young dreams were shattered in 2023 by economic hardship, unequal access to opportunities, family instability, and academic challenges. But amid despair, there is hope. 

We ignite opportunities in underprivileged communities by delivering high-quality education and forming positive action leaders.

Your generosity can spark this profound transformation.

Who we are?

Mano Amiga is a nonprofit international network of schools boasting 60 years of positive transformative impact. With a global presence spanning 9 countries, our 38 schools empower over 22,000 students annually.

Join us in shaping futures and fostering change worldwide.

Our legacy spans 25 years in Colombia as a distinctive institution of integral formation. Through our Scholarship Program, we uplift students in vulnerable conditions, covering 40% to 100% of their annual educational costs.

More than a school, we are a leadership and empowerment community project.

We tirelessly empower children to dream big, shatter mental and social barriers and craft innovative solutions together, fostering a culture of collaboration and social responsibility.
